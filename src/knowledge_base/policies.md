# Company Policies

## Refund Policy

- **30-day money-back guarantee**: Full refund for any reason within 30 days of first purchase.
- **After 30 days**: Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis at management discretion.
- **Annual plans**: Prorated refunds available within 60 days of purchase; no refunds after 60 days.
- **One-time purchases**: Non-refundable after digital delivery unless the product is defective.
- **Processing time**: 5–10 business days to the original payment method.
- **Exceptions**: Fraudulent charges are always refunded regardless of timeframe.

## SLA — Service Level Agreements

| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| Urgent | 1 hour | 4 hours |
| High | 4 hours | 1 business day |
| Medium | 8 hours | 3 business days |
| Low | 24 hours | 7 business days |

Business hours: Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM (UTC). Urgent tickets are handled 24/7.

## Escalation Policy

The following automatically trigger escalation to a senior support specialist:
- Legal threats, mentions of lawsuits or regulatory complaints
- Suspected account breach or data leak
- Billing disputes > $500
- Service outage affecting the customer for more than 48 hours
- Customer explicitly requests to speak to a manager
- Regulatory requests (GDPR data subject requests, CCPA opt-outs)

Escalated tickets are reviewed within 1 business hour during business hours.

## Communication Policy

- All support communications are logged for quality and training purposes.
- Representatives must never speculate about undisclosed features or roadmap.
- Representatives must never discuss other customers' data.
- Sensitive credentials must never be requested over email.
- Compensation (credits, discounts) above $50 must be approved by a supervisor.

## Follow-Up Policy

- If a resolution requires back-end investigation, the agent must set a follow-up within 3 business days.
- For shipping issues involving carrier claims, follow up at 5 business days.
- For refund confirmations, follow up at 10 business days if customer hasn't confirmed receipt.
- Customers who haven't responded to a support reply after 7 days receive one automated follow-up before the ticket is closed.

## Data & Privacy Policy

- Customer data is retained for 3 years post-account-deletion for legal compliance.
- GDPR subject access requests must be fulfilled within 30 days.
- GDPR deletion requests must be completed within 30 days.
- PII must never be included in internal ticket notes visible outside the support team.

## Compensation Guidelines

| Situation | Standard Compensation |
|---|---|
| Service outage > 4 hours | 1 week credit |
| Service outage > 24 hours | 1 month credit |
| Billing error (overcharge) | Full refund + 10% credit |
| Missed SLA (High/Urgent) | 1 week credit |
| Data loss due to our fault | Full refund of affected period + escalate to engineering |
