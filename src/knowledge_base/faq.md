# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Login

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and you will receive a reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support@example.com.

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A: Log in to your account, navigate to Settings → Profile → Email Address, and enter your new email. You will receive a verification link at the new address to confirm the change.

**Q: My account is locked. What should I do?**
A: Accounts are locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts for security reasons. Wait 30 minutes for the lockout to expire automatically, or contact our support team to unlock it immediately.

**Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?**
A: Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and follow the setup wizard. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS verification.

**Q: Can I have multiple users on one account?**
A: Yes. Account owners on the Business and Enterprise plans can invite team members under Settings → Team Management. Each invited user receives their own login credentials.

## Billing & Subscriptions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and PayPal. For Enterprise plans, we also accept wire transfer and purchase orders.

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
A: Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Methods and click "Add Payment Method". Your new card will be charged on your next billing cycle.

**Q: When am I billed?**
A: Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually on the anniversary of your sign-up date. You will receive an invoice by email 3 days before each charge.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings → Billing → Subscription and click "Cancel Plan". Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not charge cancellation fees.

**Q: Can I switch between plans?**
A: Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and you are charged a prorated amount. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Q: I was charged incorrectly. What should I do?**
A: Email support@example.com with your invoice number and a description of the discrepancy. Our billing team reviews disputes within 2 business days.

## Refunds & Returns

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. Refund requests submitted after 30 days are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: Contact support@example.com with your account email and the reason for the refund request. Include your invoice number if available.

**Q: Can I get a partial refund if I cancel mid-cycle?**
A: Monthly plan subscribers receive a prorated refund for unused days if they cancel within the first 30 days. Annual plan subscribers may receive a prorated refund if requested within 60 days of purchase.

## Orders & Shipping

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping: 5–7 business days. Express shipping: 2–3 business days. Overnight shipping: next business day (order must be placed before 2 PM local time). International orders: 10–21 business days depending on destination.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking email with a carrier link. You can also log in and go to Orders → Order History to view real-time tracking.

**Q: My order hasn't arrived. What should I do?**
A: First check the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email. If the tracking shows delivered but you haven't received the package, wait 24 hours (occasionally packages are marked delivered early). If still missing after 24 hours, contact us and we will file a carrier claim and reship or refund you.

**Q: Can I change or cancel an order?**
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters our fulfilment process and cannot be changed. Contact support immediately if you need to make changes.

## Technical Support

**Q: The application is slow or not loading. What should I do?**
A: 1. Clear your browser cache and cookies. 2. Try a different browser. 3. Disable browser extensions. 4. Check our status page at status.example.com for any known outages. If the issue persists, contact support with your browser version and a screenshot.

**Q: I'm getting an error message. How do I report it?**
A: Take a screenshot of the error including the error code if visible. Note the steps that led to the error. Email support@example.com with these details and your account email.

**Q: Does the application work on mobile?**
A: Yes. Our application is fully responsive and works on modern mobile browsers. We also offer native iOS and Android apps available on the App Store and Google Play.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Go to Settings → Data → Export. Select the date range and format (CSV or JSON) and click Export. You will receive a download link by email within 15 minutes.

## Privacy & Security

**Q: How is my data stored and protected?**
A: All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data is hosted in AWS data centres in the US and EU.

**Q: How do I delete my account and data?**
A: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or email support@example.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days per our privacy policy.

**Q: I think my account has been compromised. What should I do?**
A: Immediately change your password and enable 2FA. Check Settings → Security → Active Sessions and log out of all sessions. Email support@example.com urgently — we will investigate and can lock your account as a precaution.
